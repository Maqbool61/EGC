# Positioning Alternatives

The README leads with the benefit-first positioning "EGC - Give Every AI Agent the
Same Brain". The category-creation variant below was evaluated in the same
branding round and deliberately reserved for channels with a longer mental sales
cycle, where platform framing lands better than a 3-second benefit pitch.

## Reserved variant: "EGC - The Operating System for AI Memory"

- Sub: "Every AI agent. Every IDE. Every terminal. One shared memory."
- Core: "Stop treating AI sessions as disposable. EGC gives every coding agent
  persistent memory, shared context, and collective intelligence across your
  entire development workflow."
- Closing: "Your AI doesn't just remember. Your entire team gets smarter."

## Where to use it

| Channel | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Website landing page | Headline for a Platform or Enterprise section |
| Pitch deck | Vision and category-creation slide |
| Blog and thought leadership | "Why AI Memory Needs an Operating System, Not Another Tool" |
| Enterprise sales material | Architectural-differentiation slide |

Do not use it in the README: repository visitors decide in seconds and respond
to concrete benefit over category abstraction.
